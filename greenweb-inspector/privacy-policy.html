Privacy Policy for GreenWeb Inspector

Last updated: November 2025

Introduction

GreenWeb Inspector ("we," "our," "us") values the privacy of our users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the data associated with your use of our Chrome extension, "GreenWeb Inspector". By installing and using this extension, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

GreenWeb Inspector collects the following data:

  • Active Tab URL: The extension collects the URL of the current active tab in your browser to check the energy status of the website. This data is sent to an external API to retrieve the energy tier of the website.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect any personal information, such as browsing history, user credentials, or any identifiable personal data.

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used solely to:

  • Check the energy status of the websites you visit through the GreenWeb Inspector extension.
  • Provide you with information on the energy efficiency of the website you are currently viewing.

Third-Party Services

We use a third-party service to calculate the energy tier of websites. The service receives the URL of the active tab to provide an energy status. However, no personal data is shared with third parties, and we do not store any of the information transmitted through the extension.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We implement reasonable security measures to protect data from unauthorized access.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we collect about you.
  • Request deletion of your data, though we do not store personal data beyond what is necessary for the extension's operation.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, with an updated "Last Updated" date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for any changes.
